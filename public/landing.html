Raw GPU Power Meets Declarative Simplicity

GPUSims fundamentally rethinks how browser-based simulations are built. By enforcing a strict separation between your simulation's "blueprint" (a declarative TypeScript schema) and its "orchestration" (procedural WGSL execution), we give you maximum transparency, maintainability, and control over complex compute pipelines.

The Ping-Pong Workflow

Experience a revolutionary approach to writing shader code where your environment and your AI assistant work in perfect sync.

1

Architect the Schema

Describe your physics model to the AI. It generates a strict, type-safe TypeScript schema defining your memory pool and execution pipeline.

2

Auto-Generate the Skeleton

Paste the schema into GPUSims. The platform instantly parses the data and hands you a perfectly bound WGSL skeleton.

3

Implement the Logic

Pass the skeleton back to the AI to implement the core mathematical logic inside the main functions, free from boilerplate distractions.

4

Execute

Paste the finalized WGSL code into the GPUSims environment and run your live physical simulation instantly.
